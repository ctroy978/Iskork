Giants of Iskork

In the realm of Iskork, where conflict is as common as breathing, the Giants have elevated drama to an art form, turning every molehill into a mountain and every whisper into a scandalous roar. These towering figures, draped in flowing togas and adorned with laurel wreaths, strut through their cloud-kissing citadels with all the grace of a raging rhinoceros but with twice the attitude.

Giant society is a never-ending soap opera, filled with dramatic gasps, exaggerated eye-rolls, backstabbings, and tears. Social gatherings inevitably devolve into a chaotic mix of toga parties, revenge plotting, and explosive debates about who has the biggest butt.

Tribal Traits

Giants are friendly to all races and love to share their woes with anyone who will lend an ear.

Due to their dramatic nature, Giants must pay extra for mercenaries, as most find their constant drama unbearable.
